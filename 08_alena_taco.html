Preparation

STEP 1: Bring the water, taco seasoning, and onion to a boil. Add the TVP and reduce heat to low. Allow the TVP to absorb the liquid, and then add the drained black beans.

STEP 2: Cover and cook on low heat, stirring often, until the tortillas are ready – be mindful to not let the filling scorch on the bottom of the pot. Check for seasoning – add salt if needed (taco seasonings all have different salt contents so use your judgment on how much to add).

STEP 3: Heat up the tortillas in a pan with some oil, on a grill, or over your stove burner.

STEP 4: Remove the filling from the heat and build your tacos using your favorite toppings.
