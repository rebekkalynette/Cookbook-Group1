Garlic Salmon

Ingredients:

  • Salmon — Take your pick of fillets!
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Melted butter or melted ghee
  • Seasoning
  • Garnish
  • optional: tbs dijon mustard

Preparation

STEP 1: Preheat the oven to 400ºF. Prepare a baking sheet that’s large enough to fold over and seal the fish. You won’t need a rimmed baking sheet for this, as you’ll be folding the salmon with foil. In a small bowl, add lemon juice, garlic, and melted butter or melted ghee and whisk until combined.

STEP 2: Place the salmon on the prepared baking sheet and then pour the butter mixture over the salmon, careful to keep as much as the butter mixture on the salmon. Season with salt, pepper, oregano, and red pepper flakes.

STEP 3: Bake the salmon - Fold the sides of the foil over the salmon so that the salmon is fully sealed and the butter mixture cannot leak out.

STEP 4: Place the sheet pan into the oven, and bake salmon until cooked, about 12-16 minutes. Open the foil, and broil salmon for 2-3 minutes until the tops are golden. Be careful not to burn the fish by keeping a close eye on it.

STEP 5: Remove the sheet pan from the oven. Then, using a spoon, pour some of the butter sauce left in the foil onto the baked salmon and garnish before serving.
