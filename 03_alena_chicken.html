Preparation

STEP 1: Heat the oil to 180C in a deep pan or fryer. Do not fill the pan more than half full with oil.

STEP 2: In a medium bowl, beat the eggs. Add enough hot sauce so the egg mixture is bright orange (about 240ml).

STEP 3: Season the chicken with the house seasoning. Dip the seasoned chicken in the egg and then coat well in the flour.

STEP 4: Place the chicken in the preheated oil and fry until brown and crisp.
Cooking time is approximately 13-14 minutes for dark meat and 8-10 minutes for white meat.

STEP 5: House seasoning: Mix the ingredients together and store in an air-tight container for up to 6 months.
